The Story Behind Bear Arms

FUNKY FOREST – Players start in caves, sneak and shoot around luminous trees. Colors are green, blue, purple. Magical forest vibe with glowing mushrooms around the map.

DUSTIEST DESERT – Home base is a giant teepee for each team, obstacles include cacti and hilly sand dunes. Colors are red, orange and yellow and it is dusty.

MYSTICAL MOUNTAINS – Players start on opposing cliffs, battle in the valley below with falling stones as hazards (if possible, not necessary but would be cool). Magical vibes once again with main colors being pink, purple and bright blue. Maybe adding “crystals” on the mountain would enhance the map.
